The Best Movies According to Sihle

My top 5 movies of all-time

1.The Wolf of Wall Street

Based on Jordan Belfort’s life, it’s about greed, excess, and corruption in the stock market during the 1990s.

2.The founder

The story of Ray Kroc, who turned McDonald’s into a global fast-food empire, raising questions of ambition, ethics, and business power

3.The BeeKeeper

An ex-operative seeks brutal revenge after scammers destroy the life of a close friend, blending action and vigilante justice.

4.Ne Zha

A Chinese animated fantasy about a boy born with a cursed fate who must fight destiny to become a hero.

5.Despicable Me 4

Gru, the Minions, and his family return for another adventure, balancing comedy, chaos, and heart.
